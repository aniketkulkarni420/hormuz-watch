India · Hormuz exposure 🇮🇳 NSE · educational
Data · companies + sector exposure
~62% of India's crude imports flow through Hormuz. Each $10/bbl crude rise ≈ ₹85K Cr import bill increase.
Interpretation · historical pattern
Crude-importing refiners have historically underperformed during Hormuz stress episodes due to feedstock-cost compression — input crude rises while retail price is policy-anchored. Upstream producers and Indian-flag tankers have historically benefited from elevated crude and freight rates. Integrated names (RIL) typically hedge-neutral via downstream offset.
