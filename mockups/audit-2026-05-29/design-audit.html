TL;DR — measured, not asserted

  • The core IA problems from 7 days ago are still 100% present on the live site — verified with hard DOM metrics, not opinion. H1/H2/H3 = 0/0/0, 30 cards at flat visual weight, 14 distinct font sizes.
  • The biggest text on screen is "179" (a vessel count) at 30px — NOT the verdict. The eye is pulled to a secondary number while the dashboard's actual answer (NORMAL) renders smaller. Visual hierarchy points the wrong way. This is the single highest-impact fix.
  • ~73% of all visible text is 9–10px (315 of ~430 elements desktop). The dashboard reads as a dense terminal — fine for the trader persona, hostile to everyone else, and fails WCAG comfort norms.
  • A complete fix already exists and is approved-pending: redesign-v1-2026-05-22.html. This audit's recommendation is to green-light porting it, section by section, not to design something new.

§4 The 5-second test — does it pass?

FAILA trader can't answer "verdict + what's driving it" in 5 seconds
Above-the-fold dominant element: "179" (30px) — a vessel count. Verdict "NORMAL" present at top:175px but smaller, competing with the cargo ticker, the signal bar (5 tiles), and the map — all at similar weight. No single focal point. The eye has to hunt.
Fix (already prototyped): verdict-as-hero — a full-width block where the verdict is the largest thing on screen (40px), with a one-line "what changed" summary + 3-4 driver chips. See redesign-v1-2026-05-22.html §hero. Pulls the 5-second answer above everything else.

§7 Prioritized plan — approve by section

All of these are already designed in redesign-v1-2026-05-22.html. This is a port-and-verify job, not new design. Each ships with a headless self-test.

1 · P0Verdict-as-hero + heading hierarchy (H1/H2/H3)

Biggest cognitive-load relief. Verdict becomes the dominant element; sections get H2 headers (Markets / Strait activity / Geopolitics / Conditions). ~10× ROI.

2 · P1Card consolidation 30 → ~12 + section grouping

Merge Brent+WTI into one Crude card; fold weather/aircraft/seismic into one Conditions tile; move LNG/fert/CAPE to a collapsed Reference section. Group under the H2s.

3 · P1Typography scale (14 → 6 tiers) + sans/mono discipline

One modular scale; mono reserved for numbers only; kill the 8px stragglers. Lifts the 9–10px density.

4 · P1Colour semantic cleanup

Reserve green/amber/red for verdict severity ONLY; price deltas switch to blue↑/orange↓ so one colour never means two things.

5 · P2Self-explaining tiles (inline meaning)

Each number gets a micro-context ("BDTI 2088 · normal range 800–1500" or a sparkline). Recognition over recall.

6 · P2Layout — map vs analytics balance

Deferred until 1–4 land, then re-measure. Likely: map → header strip, analytics get the dominant surface. Don't resize before the hierarchy fix (more space for noise = more noise).
