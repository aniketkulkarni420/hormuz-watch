Design showcase — Hormuz Watch right sidebar

Renders every right-sidebar card across 6 data permutations so design bugs surface before they ship to the live dashboard. If anything looks broken here, the live card has the same bug. The DOM bounding-box check at the bottom is a tripwire for overflow regressions — red lines = act.

Acceptance checklist — scan every card

  • Counts and percentages always in the SAME visual position across every row of a card
  • No text spills outside its container (column, bar, tile)
  • Bars / fills never exceed 100% width of their track
  • Long labels truncate with ellipsis or wrap — they never bleed into neighbours
  • Empty / loading / stale states render explicitly (no "0" disguised as fresh data)
  • Colors used semantically: green = good, amber = warn, red = bad, blue = info, muted = absent
  • Numbers and units always paired (no orphan "42" without "transits", no "$" without value)
  • Self-test panel at the bottom shows all clear
